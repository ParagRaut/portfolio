Working in Enterprise

Working in enterprise has it’s own benefits and detriments. On one side you get exposed to a huge company wide technical architecture and good amount of business processes and on other side once you get acquainted with it, you are mostly locked to rigid/non-bleeding edge/legacy/boring/meh/same o' same o'/you name it technology stack. Specifically for a tinkerer like me it gets booooooring real soon. Then the real struggle starts, you get stuck in a vicious circle of learning a new technology to keep up with the ever-changing world of tech and then not being able to apply it anywhere in your work (literally anywhere), and eventually loosing grip on whatever I have learned.
